Design Intent

Slow Committee burst

Lower opening gold and weaponry, then make execution and King Louis cards more expensive.

Shape Bourgeoisie arc

Give a cleaner start, then raise endgame loyalty and National Freedom thresholds.

Raise Peasant floor

More food, more food income, cheaper farm, and a slightly easier food objective.

Keep Nobles viable

Extra gold and weaponry plus lower economic and military objective targets.
